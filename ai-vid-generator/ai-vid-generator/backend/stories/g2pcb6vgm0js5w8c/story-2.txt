Discover the innovative and colorful Tubo Hotel in Colombia, made from upcycled sewer pipes. Enjoy a desert oasis with air-conditioned rooms and a shared swimming pool. In Canada, experience the Free Spirit Spheres, suspended among conifers like oversized Christmas baubles. These eco-friendly pods offer a unique way to immerse yourself in nature. For a more serene experience, visit KINN Capsule in Singapore, where minimalist design meets Zen tranquility.
