The Green Chile Cheeseburger Trail in New Mexico features 66 standout spots, including Sparky’s Burgers, Buckhorn Tavern, and Mineshaft Tavern. Each offers a unique take on the GCCB, from Sparky’s classic version to Buckhorn’s towering burger and Mineshaft’s gourmet affair. The trail celebrates the rich history and diverse flavors of the GCCB, a dish deeply rooted in New Mexico's culture. Whether mild or spicy, roasted or deep-fried, the green chile cheeseburger remains a testament to the state's love for this fiery ingredient.
