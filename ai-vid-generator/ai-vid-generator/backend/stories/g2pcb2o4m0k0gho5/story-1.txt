Apple and Google Wallets are set to replace hotel key cards. Only 14% of guests currently use digital keys, but experts say phone apps are more secure and convenient. Major hotel chains are adopting this technology, though some guests still prefer plastic cards. Security concerns exist, but digital keys offer quick updates. The transition is costly, and not all hotels will switch soon. Digital keys enhance convenience, but plastic cards remain relevant. The future of hotel access is evolving, balancing security, cost, and user preference.
