Discover eight of the world's most extraordinary tiny hotel rooms. From Colombia's upcycled sewer pipes to spheres suspended in the sky in Canada, capsule hotels have been reinvented for a new generation of travelers. High hotel room rates and the boom in solo travel have supercharged this low-cost concept, offering budget travelers more privacy than a hostel and more comfort than camping. Unique experiences and shareable stories for social media are guaranteed. Explore these innovative and colorful places that offer everything you need to rest in a natural environment of fresh air and vegetation.
