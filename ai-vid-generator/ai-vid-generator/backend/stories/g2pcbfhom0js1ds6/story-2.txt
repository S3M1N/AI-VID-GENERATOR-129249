Nine Hours in Japan offers a sleep laboratory experience with sleep data tracking. Natura Vive in Peru features transparent sleeping pods on a cliff face. Tubo Hotel La Tatacoa in Colombia offers air-conditioned rooms made from upcycled sewer pipes. Free Spirit Spheres in Canada are suspended among conifers, offering an immersive nature experience. KINN Capsule in Singapore provides a Zen take on the capsule concept with calming interiors. Each of these hotels offers a unique and memorable stay.
