F1 racer Pierre Gasly loves living in fashion-forward Milan. Here are his top weekend picks, from shopping in Quadrilatero della moda to walking his puppy in Parco Sempione. For Gasly, a pilot for the BWT Alpine F1 team, everyday life is as fast-paced as a lap on the circuit. In 2019, Gasly settled in Milan, Italy's stylish financial capital. He loves the city's combination of fashion, food, and Formula One culture. Gasly's favorite spots include the Duomo, Navigli, and the Quadrilatero della moda for shopping. For dining, he recommends Osteria da Fortunata and Beefbar.
