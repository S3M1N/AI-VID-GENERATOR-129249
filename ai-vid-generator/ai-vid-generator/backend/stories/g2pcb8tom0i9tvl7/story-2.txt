Gasly's favorite spots include the Duomo di Milano, a Gothic marvel, and the Navigli nightlife quarter. He also enjoys shopping in the Quadrilatero della moda, Milan's fashion district. For dining, Gasly recommends Osteria da Fortunata for its incredible pasta and Beefbar for a swanky steakhouse experience. For a romantic night out, he suggests La Gioia in Brera, a refined seafood restaurant. When it comes to nightlife, Gasly prefers elegant and low-key spots like the Bar at Ralph Lauren and La Bullona.
